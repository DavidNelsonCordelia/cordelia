How It Works

Three user types with unique interfaces come together to create the smooth operation of your online store, enabling you to provide pick-up and delivery service to your customers.

calendar_today Administrator Administrator interface allows updates to the online inventory, schedule management, and item photo customization list Employee Your employees utilize an intuitive tracking system to fill customer orders efficiently shopping_basket Customer Easy online ordering of items in your store for pick-up or delivery

Features designed specifically for co-ops

By-donation Service Fee Many co-ops are torn between wanting to offer curbside and delivery free of service fees to ensure accessibility for all, and needing to make the services financially sustainable. So, we created the by-donation service fee, which allows your customers to contribute whatever amount they are able when making an order. Round Up at Checkout If your store offers a round-up program, we can configure Cloud Co-op to allow your online customers to participate. During checkout they will be able to opt-in to round up their total, just like they would in the store. You can even update the recipient of these donations as it changes from month to month. Special Orders Cloud Co-op can be configured to offer anything your store sells via pre-order or special order. These could be seasonal items, like pies or turkeys, one-off creations like special occasion cakes, or perhaps catering dishes...anything you want to collect payment for up front and schedule a pickup date at a later time, we can handle! Owner Applications and Equity Payments Member-owners are what make a co-op a co-op, so it only makes sense to incorporate ownership into your online-ordering system. We can configure your store to allow customers to apply for ownership and pay equity conveniently online. It's as simple as customers filling out a form and entering payment information. Then, we email you their application information so you can reconcile it with any ownership management system! Analytical Tools The Lost Sales Report provides useful feedback to assist in purchasing and production decisions by recording the dollar value and number of times items are ordered online but found to be out of stock during order fulfilment. Popular Order Times is a handy chart which uses your store's history of recent orders to track ordering patterns over time! This can help you understand what days and times of day are most popular for online orders and anticipate when you will need extra staff on hand.
