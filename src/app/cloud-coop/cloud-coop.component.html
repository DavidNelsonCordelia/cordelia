Cloud Co-op by Cordelia
Cloud Co-op is an online shopping platform designed specifically with co-ops in mind.

Features

Smooth shopping experience Our simple, modern user interface makes shopping pleasant and easy for your customers. Your brand We style the online store with your branding to create a familiar environment to shoppers. Responsive web application Accessible from any wifi enabled device directly through the browser; no need to download anything. Data integration Our experience with industry standard technology, including Catapult, allows us to smoothly upload your inventory and keep it current with automatic updates. Secure transactions & storage Modern transaction processing and storage of user data keeps you and your customers safe. Long term support Because we utilize established, industry standard software including Stripe, Amazon, and Angular, you never need to worry about your store degrading over time.

How It Works

Three user types with unique interfaces come together to create the smooth operation of your online store, enabling you to provide pick-up and delivery service to your customers.

calendar_today Administrator Administrator interface allows updates to the online inventory, schedule management, and item photo customization list Employee Your employees utilize an intuitive tracking system to fill customer orders efficiently shopping_basket Customer Easy online ordering of items in your store for pick-up or delivery
