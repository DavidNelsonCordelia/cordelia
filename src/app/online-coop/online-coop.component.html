Online Co-op by Cordelia
Online co-op is a digital shopping platform designed specifically with co-op's in mind.

How it works

Three user types with unique interfaces come together to create the smooth operation of your online store, enabling you to provide pick-up and delivery service to your customers.

calendar_today Administrator Administrator interface allows custom pick-up and delivery scheduling list Employee Your employees utilize an intuitive tracking system to fill customer orders efficiently shopping_basket Customer Easy online ordering of items in your store for pick-up or delivery
